# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

**Be respectful.** Disagreements happen — focus on the idea, not the person.

**Be constructive.** If something is wrong, suggest a fix. If something is good, say so.

**Be direct.** Say what you mean. Don't waste people's time.

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behaviour can be reported to the maintainers. We will review and respond appropriately.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, PRs, discussions.
